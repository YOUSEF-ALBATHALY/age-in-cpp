# Your Age In Program

This is a simple C++ program that calculates your age in:

- Days
- Hours
- Minutes
- Seconds

## Sample Output

Your age in years : 22 Years
Your age in days : 8030 Days
Your age in hours : 192720 h
Your age in minutes : 11563200 m
Your age in seconds : 693792000 s
